# PMID 17713731

## Citation

Vuksic T, Zoricic I, et al. *Stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 in trials for inflammatory bowel disease ... heals ileoileal anastomosis in the rat.* Surg Today. 2007.

## Why it matters

This paper is important historically because it ties the preclinical program to named inflammatory bowel disease trial codes.

## What it says

The study itself is a rat anastomosis paper. In the background, it states that BPC-157 had been shown to be safe in clinical trials for inflammatory bowel disease.

## Use in project summaries

Use it to show that claims of earlier human-trial activity exist inside the indexed literature.

## Constraint

The paper does not publish human trial outcomes. Treat its human-trial statements as historical pointers, not conclusive human evidence.
