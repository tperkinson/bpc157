# USADA BPC-157 Guidance

## Citation

USADA. *BPC-157: Experimental Peptide Creates Risk for Athletes.*

## Why it matters

This is the cleanest official explanation of current athlete-facing status.

## What it says

USADA states that BPC-157 is prohibited under WADA category S0 Unapproved Substances and is not approved for human clinical use by any global regulatory authority.

## Use in project summaries

Use it for current sports-governance and public-risk language.
