# PMID 9403790

## Citation

Seiwerth S, Sikiric P, et al. *BPC 157's effect on healing.* J Physiol Paris. 1997.

## Why it matters

This is one of the earliest indexed papers in the library and helps anchor the history of the BPC-157 literature.

## What it says

The paper reports improved granulation tissue, collagen formation, angiogenesis, and tensile-strength development in multiple rat healing models.

## Use in project summaries

Use this source to support the claim that BPC-157 has been studied in wound-healing models since the 1990s.

## Constraint

Do not use this paper to imply proven human efficacy.
