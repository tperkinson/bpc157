# PMID 20225319

## Citation

Cerovecki T, Bojanic I, et al. *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 (PL 14736) improves ligament healing in the rat.* J Orthop Res. 2010.

## Why it matters

This is a commonly cited musculoskeletal paper behind many modern repair claims.

## What it says

The authors report improved functional, biomechanical, macroscopic, and histological outcomes in a rat medial collateral ligament model after intraperitoneal, oral, or topical administration.

## Use in project summaries

Use it as an example of the strongest type of BPC-157 musculoskeletal evidence currently available: animal data.

## Constraint

This should not be presented as proof that BPC-157 repairs ligaments in humans.
