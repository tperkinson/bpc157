# PMID 21030672

## Citation

Chang CH, Tsai WC, Lin MS, Hsu YH, Pang JH. *The promoting effect of pentadecapeptide BPC 157 on tendon healing involves tendon outgrowth, cell survival, and cell migration.* J Appl Physiol. 2011.

## Why it matters

This is a useful mechanism paper outside the core Croatian author cluster.

## What it says

The study links BPC-157 exposure to tendon fibroblast migration, spreading, survival under oxidative stress, and FAK/paxillin signaling changes.

## Use in project summaries

Use it to describe mechanistic plausibility, not clinical proof.
