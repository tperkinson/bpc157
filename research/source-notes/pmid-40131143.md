# PMID 40131143

## Citation

Lee E, Burgess K. *Safety of Intravenous Infusion of BPC157 in Humans: A Pilot Study.* Altern Ther Health Med. 2025.

## Why it matters

This is the clearest published human safety paper found in this pass.

## What it says

Two adults received one-hour infusions of BPC-157 up to 20 mg, and the paper reports no measurable adverse effects in the reported biomarkers or vital signs.

## Use in project summaries

Use it to say there is published human safety reporting, but only at an extremely preliminary level.

## Constraint

The sample size is two. This does not establish general safety.
