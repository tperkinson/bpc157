# PMID 40756949

## Citation

Vasireddi N, Hahamyan H, et al. *Emerging Use of BPC-157 in Orthopaedic Sports Medicine: A Systematic Review.* HSS J. 2025.

## Why it matters

This is the best high-level evidence snapshot for musculoskeletal use in the current library.

## What it says

The review identified 36 included studies from 1993 to 2024, with 35 preclinical studies and 1 clinical study.

## Use in project summaries

Use the 35-to-1 ratio to explain the mismatch between public enthusiasm and the actual clinical evidence base.
