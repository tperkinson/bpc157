# PMID 34324435

## Citation

Lee E, Padgett B. *Intra-Articular Injection of BPC 157 for Multiple Types of Knee Pain.* Altern Ther Health Med. 2021.

## Why it matters

This is one of the very few published human studies in the library.

## What it says

It is a retrospective chart review with follow-up by phone. The paper reports improvement in most contacted patients after intra-articular BPC-157, alone or with TB4.

## Use in project summaries

Use it only as limited observational evidence.

## Constraint

Emphasize the lack of controls, the small sample, the mixed interventions, and the absence of validated outcome measures.
