# PMID 40005999

## Citation

Jozwiak M, Bauer M, Kamysz W, Kleczkowska P. *Multifunctionality and Possible Medical Application of the BPC 157 Peptide-Literature and Patent Review.* Pharmaceuticals (Basel). 2025.

## Why it matters

This is a recent open-access review that is useful for orientation and for the patent/history layer.

## What it says

The review summarizes broad preclinical claims, touches on safety and mechanism, and explicitly notes the absence of sufficient clinical evidence for routine medical approval.

## Use in project summaries

Use it as a synthesis source and pointer to patent activity, not as a substitute for primary studies.
