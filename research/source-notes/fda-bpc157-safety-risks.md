# FDA BPC-157 Safety-Risk Entry

## Citation

FDA. *Safety Risks Associated with Certain Bulk Drug Substances Nominated for Use in Compounding.*

## Why it matters

This is an official regulatory source directly relevant to current safety and compounding claims.

## What it says

FDA flags BPC-157 for immunogenicity and impurity concerns and notes nonclinical safety signals including possible male reproductive and protumorigenic risks.

## Use in project summaries

Use it to counter unsupported claims that BPC-157 has a settled safety profile.
