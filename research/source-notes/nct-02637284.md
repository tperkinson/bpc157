# NCT02637284

## Citation

ClinicalTrials.gov. *PCO-02 - Safety and Pharmacokinetics Trial.*

## Why it matters

This is the clearest official registry evidence in the library for a formal human BPC-157 phase I study.

## What it says

The record describes a randomized placebo-controlled pilot study in healthy volunteers evaluating oral BPC-157 safety and pharmacokinetics. The page shows unknown status and no posted results.

## Use in project summaries

Use it to show that at least one formal human trial was registered publicly.

## Constraint

Do not imply the trial succeeded or produced positive findings. The public record does not provide results.
