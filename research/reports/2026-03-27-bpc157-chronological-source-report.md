# BPC-157: Chronological Source Report

Date: 2026-03-27

## Purpose

This report is a chronological inventory of every source currently accepted into the BPC-157 library. Each bullet gives the year, the source identifier, and a short note on what the source covers.

It is meant to sit between the shorter summary reports and the raw source catalog. The goal is completeness, not brevity.

## Chronological source list

### 1990s

- 1993. PMID 8298609, *A new gastric juice peptide, BPC. An overview of the stomach-stress-organoprotection hypothesis and beneficial effects of BPC.* Covers the foundational overview that frames BPC around stomach-stress-organoprotection before the later BPC-157 literature expands.
- 1993. PMID 7901724, *Hepatoprotective effect of BPC 157, a 15-amino acid peptide, on liver lesions induced by either restraint stress or bile duct and hepatic artery ligation or CCl4 administration. A comparative study with dopamine agonists and somatostatin.* Covers one of the earliest direct BPC-157 papers, extending the literature into hepatoprotection models.
- 1997. PMID 9403790, *BPC 157's effect on healing.* Covers one of the earliest indexed healing papers, with rat-model results on granulation tissue, collagen, angiogenesis, and tensile strength.
- 1997. PMID 9298922, *The influence of a novel pentadecapeptide, BPC 157, on N(G)-nitro-L-arginine methylester and L-arginine effects on stomach mucosa integrity and blood pressure.* Covers stomach-mucosa integrity and blood-pressure effects under nitric-oxide-pathway manipulation.
- 1997. PMID 9403788, *The influence of BPC 157 on nitric oxide agonist and antagonist induced lesions in broiler chicks.* Covers a non-rodent lesion model tied to nitric-oxide agonist and antagonist exposure.
- 1999. PMID 10499368, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 attenuates disturbances induced by neuroleptics: the effect on catalepsy and gastric ulcers in mice and rats.* Covers neuroleptic-induced catalepsy alongside gastric-ulcer outcomes in animal models.
- 1999. PMID 10672996, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 attenuates gastric lesions induced by alloxan in rats and mice.* Covers gastric-lesion outcomes and reinforces the early gastrointestinal cytoprotection theme.
- 1999. PMID 10707891, *The effect of a novel pentadecapeptide BPC 157 on development of tolerance and physical dependence following repeated administration of diazepam.* Covers tolerance and physical dependence after repeated diazepam exposure.

### 2000s

- 2000. PMID 10791689, *The antidepressant effect of an antiulcer pentadecapeptide BPC 157 in Porsolt's test and chronic unpredictable stress in rats. A comparison with antidepressants.* Covers antidepressant-like effects in rat behavioral models.
- 2001. PMID 11718984, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 cream improves burn-wound healing and attenuates burn-gastric lesions in mice.* Covers topical burn-wound healing together with burn-related gastric-lesion outcomes.
- 2001. PMID 11595456, *Portal hypertension and liver lesions in chronically alcohol drinking rats prevented and reversed by stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 (PL-10, PLD-116), and propranolol, but not ranitidine.* Covers alcohol-associated portal hypertension and liver lesions in rats.
- 2002. PMID 11978191, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 attenuates chronic amphetamine-induced behavior disturbances.* Covers chronic amphetamine-related behavior disturbance models.
- 2003. PMID 14554208, *Gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 accelerates healing of transected rat Achilles tendon and in vitro stimulates tendocytes growth.* Covers Achilles tendon healing in rats and tendocyte-growth effects in vitro.
- 2004. PMID 15153646, *Doxorubicine-congestive heart failure-increased big endothelin-1 plasma concentration: reversal by amlodipine, losartan, and gastric pentadecapeptide BPC157 in rat and mouse.* Covers doxorubicin-associated heart-failure and endothelin-related vascular injury contexts.
- 2004. PMID 15531385, *Effects of pentadecapeptide BPC157 on regional serotonin synthesis in the rat brain: alpha-methyl-L-tryptophan autoradiographic measurements.* Covers regional serotonin-synthesis effects in rat brain.
- 2004. PMID 15381050, *The influence of gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 on acute and chronic ethanol administration in mice.* Covers acute and chronic ethanol-exposure models in mice.
- 2005. PMID 15840402, *Gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 effective against serotonin syndrome in rats.* Covers serotonin-syndrome outcomes in rats.
- 2006. PMID 16583442, *Achilles detachment in rat and stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157: Promoted tendon-to-bone healing and opposed corticosteroid aggravation.* Covers tendon-to-bone healing and corticosteroid-aggravated injury in a musculoskeletal animal model.
- 2007. PMID 17713731, *Stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 in trials for inflammatory bowel disease (PL-10, PLD-116, PL14736, Pliva, Croatia) heals ileoileal anastomosis in the rat.* Covers rat anastomosis healing and also documents the literature's recurring references to earlier IBD trial programs.
- 2009. PMID 20388962, *Gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 counteracts morphine-induced analgesia in mice.* Covers morphine-induced analgesia in a mouse pharmacology model.
- 2009. PMID 20388964, *Modulatory effect of gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 on angiogenesis in muscle and tendon healing.* Covers angiogenesis in muscle and tendon healing.

### 2010s

- 2010. PMID 20190676, *Impact of pentadecapeptide BPC 157 on muscle healing impaired by systemic corticosteroid application.* Covers muscle-healing outcomes when repair is impaired by systemic corticosteroid exposure.
- 2010. PMID 20225319, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 (PL 14736) improves ligament healing in the rat.* Covers rat medial collateral ligament healing and is one of the most-cited musculoskeletal papers in the library.
- 2011. PMID 21645505, *Ibuprofen hepatic encephalopathy, hepatomegaly, gastric lesion and gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 in rats.* Covers ibuprofen-related hepatic, gastric, and encephalopathy outcomes in rats.
- 2011. PMID 21295044, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 and its effects on a NSAID toxicity model: diclofenac-induced gastrointestinal, liver, and encephalopathy lesions.* Covers diclofenac-related gastrointestinal, liver, and encephalopathy lesions.
- 2011. PMID 21030672, *The promoting effect of pentadecapeptide BPC 157 on tendon healing involves tendon outgrowth, cell survival, and cell migration.* Covers tendon outgrowth, fibroblast survival, migration, and FAK/paxillin signaling in a mechanistic tendon-healing study.
- 2012. PMID 21840572, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 reduces bleeding time and thrombocytopenia after amputation in rats treated with heparin, warfarin or aspirin.* Covers bleeding time and thrombocytopenia in anticoagulant- or antiplatelet-treated rats.
- 2013. PMID 24304574, *Stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 heals cysteamine-colitis and colon-colon-anastomosis and counteracts cuprizone brain injuries and motor disability.* Covers a multisystem animal paper spanning colitis, anastomosis, brain injury, and motor disability models.
- 2014. PMID 23782145, *BPC 157 and blood vessels.* Covers vascular and endothelial themes that recur across later BPC-157 claims.
- 2014. PMID 23755725, *Stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157-NO-system relation.* Covers the nitric-oxide-system framing that appears repeatedly across the literature.
- 2015. NCT02637284, *PCO-02 - Safety and Pharmacokinetics Trial.* Covers a registered phase I randomized placebo-controlled oral safety and pharmacokinetic study in healthy volunteers, with no posted results.
- 2016. PMID 27895400, *Esophagogastric anastomosis in rats: Improved healing by BPC 157 and L-arginine, aggravated by L-NAME.* Covers esophagogastric anastomosis healing in relation to nitric-oxide pathway modifiers.
- 2016. PMID 27815173, *Stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 and bupivacaine.* Covers a bupivacaine-related injury or pharmacology context.
- 2017. PMID 29358856, *Stable gastric pentadecapeptide BPC 157 in the treatment of colitis and ischemia and reperfusion in rats: New insights.* Covers colitis together with ischemia-reperfusion in a detailed gastrointestinal preclinical paper.
- 2018. PMID 30279308, *An endogeous defensive concept, renewed cytoprotection/adaptive cytoprotection: intra(per)-oral/intragastric strong alcohol in rat. Involvement of pentadecapeptide BPC 157 and nitric oxide system.* Covers cytoprotection, adaptive cytoprotection, alcohol injury, and nitric-oxide-system themes.
- 2018. PMID 29998800, *BPC 157 and Standard Angiogenic Growth Factors. Gastrointestinal Tract Healing, Lessons from Tendon, Ligament, Muscle and Bone Healing.* Covers an integrative review connecting angiogenic growth factors, GI healing, and musculoskeletal repair themes.
- 2018. PMID 29898649, *BPC157 as Potential Agent Rescuing from Cancer Cachexia.* Covers cancer-cachexia and systemic catabolic-injury contexts.
- 2018. PMID 29879879, *Novel Cytoprotective Mediator, Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157. Vascular Recruitment and Gastrointestinal Tract Healing.* Covers cytoprotection, vascular recruitment, and gastrointestinal-tract healing in review form.
- 2019. PMID 30915550, *Gastric pentadecapeptide body protection compound BPC 157 and its role in accelerating musculoskeletal soft tissue healing.* Covers the musculoskeletal soft-tissue-healing argument in a later review.
- 2019. PMID 31976029, *Intragastric Application of Aspirin, Clopidogrel, Cilostazol, and BPC 157 in Rats: Platelet Aggregation and Blood Clot.* Covers platelet aggregation and blood-clot outcomes in rats.

### 2020s

- 2020. PMID 32445447, *BPC 157 Rescued NSAID-cytotoxicity Via Stabilizing Intestinal Permeability and Enhancing Cytoprotection.* Covers NSAID-related cytotoxicity, intestinal permeability, and cytoprotection.
- 2020. PMID 32547687, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 resolves Pringle maneuver in rats, both ischemia and reperfusion.* Covers liver ischemia-reperfusion injury in the Pringle maneuver model.
- 2020. PMID 32226643, *Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 resolves suprahepatic occlusion of the inferior caval vein, Budd-Chiari syndrome model in rats.* Covers a Budd-Chiari syndrome rat model and broader vascular or hemodynamic claims.
- 2021. PMID 34324435, *Intra-Articular Injection of BPC 157 for Multiple Types of Knee Pain.* Covers a small retrospective clinic report on mixed knee-pain presentations after intra-articular BPC-157 use.
- 2021. PMID 34680419, *Robert's Intragastric Alcohol-Induced Gastric Lesion Model as an Escalated General Peripheral and Central Syndrome, Counteracted by the Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157.* Covers alcohol-induced gastric-lesion and broader peripheral or central syndrome framing.
- 2021. PMID 34267654, *Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 and Wound Healing.* Covers a broad wound-healing review across repair models.
- 2021. PMID 34829776, *Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 as a Therapy for the Disable Myotendinous Junctions in Rats.* Covers disabled myotendinous junctions in rats.
- 2021. PMID 34572392, *Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 Heals Established Vesicovaginal Fistula and Counteracts Stone Formation in Rats.* Covers vesicovaginal fistula healing and stone-formation outcomes in rats.
- 2022. PMID 35884767, *BPC 157, L-NAME, L-Arginine, NO-Relation, in the Suited Rat Ketamine Models Resembling "Negative-Like" Symptoms of Schizophrenia.* Covers ketamine-model negative-like symptoms and nitric-oxide-system framing.
- 2022. PMID 36588717, *Pharmacokinetics, distribution, metabolism, and excretion of body-protective compound 157, a potential drug for treating various wounds, in rats and dogs.* Covers pharmacokinetics, distribution, metabolism, and excretion in animal models.
- 2022. PMID 36551977, *Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 and Striated, Smooth, and Heart Muscle.* Covers claims around striated, smooth, and heart-muscle contexts in review form.
- 2022. PMID 35449779, *The anti-nociceptive effect of BPC-157 on the incisional pain model in rats.* Covers anti-nociceptive effects in an incisional pain model.
- 2023. USADA guidance, *BPC-157: Experimental Peptide Creates Risk for Athletes.* Covers anti-doping status and athlete-use risk, stating that BPC-157 is prohibited under WADA category S0 and not approved for human clinical use.
- 2024. FDA safety page, *Safety Risks Associated with Certain Bulk Drug Substances Nominated for Use in Compounding: BPC-157 entry.* Covers FDA safety concerns around immunogenicity, impurity complexity, and nonclinical risk signals.
- 2024. PMID 38675421, *The Stable Gastric Pentadecapeptide BPC 157 Pleiotropic Beneficial Activity and Its Possible Relations with Neurotransmitter Activity.* Covers neurotransmitter and neuropharmacology-oriented mechanism claims in review form.
- 2025. PMID 40756949, *Emerging Use of BPC-157 in Orthopaedic Sports Medicine: A Systematic Review.* Covers the clearest recent synthesis showing that the evidence base remains overwhelmingly preclinical.
- 2025. PMID 40005999, *Multifunctionality and Possible Medical Application of the BPC 157 Peptide-Literature and Patent Review.* Covers a broad literature and patent review that also acknowledges the lack of sufficient human clinical evidence for approval.
- 2025. PMID 40131143, *Safety of Intravenous Infusion of BPC157 in Humans: A Pilot Study.* Covers a two-person intravenous human safety pilot.
- 2026. PMID 41490200, *Therapeutic Peptides in Orthopaedics: Applications, Challenges, and Future Directions.* Covers a broader orthopaedic peptide review that treats BPC-157 as promising preclinically but still lacking clinical-trial support.

## Reading notes

- The list is chronological, not quality-ranked.
- Most sources are preclinical or review material.
- The human-facing record in the chronology remains narrow: one registry record and two small published human papers.
- The chronology is useful as a map of the literature, but it does not change the core conclusion that human evidence remains thin.
